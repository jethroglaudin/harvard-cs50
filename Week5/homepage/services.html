Office Building by DCS Solutions ®

Routine office cleaning is essential to keeping employees efficient, motivated and most of all from getting sick.


Our Cleaning Service Professionals are highly-trained and experienced at cleaning all types of offices including small commercial offices to large corporate office buildings & multi-tenant facilities. Our focus is not only to ensure that your office appears clean for you employees and visitors, but that critical surfaces are thoroughly cleaned and disinfected to prevent the spread of germs and illness throghout your office.

According to recent studies, kitchen faucet handles, microwaves and refrigerator doors, keyboards and telephones are among the most contaminated surfaces in an office building. With DCS you can rest assured that we use our extensive industry knowledge and experience to ensure that our Service Professionals are not only concentrating on cleaning the areas commonly thought to be dirty (i.e. restrooms) but also the unsuspecting areas that have proven to be germ-filled.
